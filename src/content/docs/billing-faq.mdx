# Billing FAQ

Quick answers to common billing questions.

---

## How do I upgrade or downgrade?

Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** and choose a new plan. Changes take effect immediately.

---

## How do I cancel?

You can cancel from **Dashboard → Settings → Billing**. Your plan remains active until the end of the billing period.

---

## Where are my invoices?

Invoices are available in the billing portal. Open **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** and click **Manage subscription**.

---

## Can I change my payment method?

Yes. Use the billing portal to update your card or payment method.

---

## Do you offer refunds?

Contact support@chatiq.io and include your account email and invoice ID.

---

## Need help?
